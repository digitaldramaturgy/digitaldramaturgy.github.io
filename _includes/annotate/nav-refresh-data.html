Change Play Data
Use a Published Google Sheet
  1. In your Google Sheet, ensure your annotated play is in the first sheet
  2. Click "File", "Share", and select "Publish to the Web"
  3. On the popup modal, select "Entire Document" and "Comma-separated values (.csv)"
  4. Click "Publish" button and copy the provided link
  5. Paste the link in the form below and click Submit

NOTE: You can also add a parameter to the end of the site like: https://thecdil.github.io/dramabase/?play={YOUR GOOGLE SHEET LINK HERE}

Use Metadata CSV from Computer
  1. On your Google Sheet, click "File" and select "Download as Comma-separated values"
  2. The file should download to your computer's Downloads folder
  3. Click "Choose file" below and navigate to select the downloaded CSV
