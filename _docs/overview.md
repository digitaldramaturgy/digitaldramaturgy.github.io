# Platform Overview

Digital Dramaturgy is a platform designed to help you publish annotated and/or edited playscripts. It can be used as a collaborative educational tool in a classroom or as a dramaturgical aid and preservation measure for dramatic presentations.

Built on the foundation of [CollectionBuilder-CSV](https://collectionbuilder.github.io/), a flexible template for creating digital collection and exhibit websites, Digital Dramaturgy offers a specialized framework for working with playscripts.

The platform follows a simple three-part workflow:

1.  **Prepare your script:** Start by building your annotated playscript using a Google Sheet.
2.  **Test your script:** Publish your Google Sheet to the web and connect it to the main Digital Dramaturgy site to see how it appears.
3.  **Create your own site:** Make a copy of the Digital Dramaturgy GitHub repository and replace the base information with your own to publish your independent site.

This documentation will guide you through each of these steps.
