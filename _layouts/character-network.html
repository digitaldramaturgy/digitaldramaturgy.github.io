Character Network Visualization

This interactive network visualization shows character relationships based on shared scenes and acts. Node size represents scene appearances, while connections indicate shared scenes.

Character Classifications
  • Major Characters Primary: Top 15% by combined scene presence and dialogue share
  • Supporting Characters Success: 8-15% range - important secondary roles
  • Minor Characters Info: 2-8% range - moderate presence in the play
  • Background Characters Secondary: Under 2% - minimal speaking roles
How to Use
  • Click and drag nodes to rearrange the network
  • Click on a character to see detailed information
  • Use the zoom controls or mouse wheel to zoom in/out
  • Hover over connections to see shared scene information
  • Use the control panel to adjust visualization settings
Connection Strength

Character connections are determined by the number of scenes two characters share together.

Connection Categories:
  • Strong Connections: Characters who share 3 or more scenes together
  • Medium Connections: Characters who share exactly 2 scenes together
  • Weak Connections: Characters who share only 1 scene together
